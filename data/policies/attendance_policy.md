# Attendance Policy

## Working Hours
- Standard working hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Monday–Friday).
- Flexible start times available between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM.
- Core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (all employees must be available).

## Remote Work
- Up to 3 days per week remote work with manager approval.
- Fully remote arrangements require VP approval.
- Remote employees must maintain reliable internet connectivity.

## Time Tracking
- All employees must log hours worked daily.
- Overtime (>40 hours/week) requires prior manager approval.
- Overtime paid at 1.5x base rate for non-exempt employees.

## Absenteeism
- Unscheduled absences exceeding 3 per quarter trigger a review.
- Chronic absenteeism (>10 unscheduled days/year) may lead to disciplinary action.
