# Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
- Employees accrue 2.5 days of annual leave per month (30 days per year).
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 15 days to the next calendar year.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance.
- Emergency leave may be approved retroactively with manager approval.

## Sick Leave
- 12 days of fully paid sick leave per year.
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- Unused sick leave does not carry forward.

## Parental Leave
- 26 weeks of maternity leave at full pay.
- 4 weeks of paternity leave at full pay.
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks at full pay.

## Unpaid Leave
- Up to 30 days of unpaid leave per year with manager approval.
- Unpaid leave does not affect benefits or tenure calculations.
