# Compliance Policy

## Code of Conduct
- All employees must complete annual compliance training.
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed within 30 days.
- Gifts from vendors exceeding $100 must be reported.

## Data Privacy
- Employee data must not be shared with unauthorized parties.
- Access to HR records requires role-based authorization.
- Data retention: 7 years after employment ends.

## Termination
- Voluntary termination requires 2 weeks written notice.
- Involuntary termination requires HR + Legal approval.
- Exit interviews are mandatory for all departures.
- Final paycheck must be issued within 10 business days.

## Audits
- Quarterly compliance audits are mandatory.
- Audit findings must be remediated within 30 days.
- Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
