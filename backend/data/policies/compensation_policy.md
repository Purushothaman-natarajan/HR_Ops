# Compensation Policy

## Salary Structure
- Salaries are reviewed annually in Q1.
- Merit increases range from 3% to 10% based on performance rating.
- Promotion increases range from 10% to 20%.
- Minimum salary: $35,000 per year (full-time equivalent).

## Bonuses
- Annual performance bonus: up to 15% of base salary.
- Spot awards: up to $2,000 for exceptional contributions.
- Referral bonus: $3,000 for successful candidate referrals.

## Adjustments
- All salary adjustments require HR manager approval.
- Off-cycle adjustments require VP-level approval.
- Retroactive adjustments are not permitted without compliance sign-off.

## Equity
- Equity grants are determined by role level and reviewed annually.
- Vesting schedule: 4-year with 1-year cliff.
